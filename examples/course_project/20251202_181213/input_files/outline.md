# ChatGPT Pro Power User: No-Code Productivity and Analysis

### Overview:
This intensive 24-hour course is designed for cross-functional professionals who want to master ChatGPT’s advanced no-code productivity and analysis features. No technical background is required. Across eight scaffolded modules, participants will progress from prompt and context fundamentals to advanced applications in data analysis, web research, document intelligence, image generation, and custom GPT workflows. The course emphasizes hands-on labs, artifact-based assessments, and governance best practices, enabling learners to confidently integrate ChatGPT Pro into real-world business scenarios.

---

## Prerequisites

- **Accounts:** ChatGPT Plus or Teams/Enterprise with ADA, Browse, Vision, Image Generation, Custom Instructions/Memory, Custom GPT Builder, and Teams/Enterprise features enabled (or access to an instructor demo tenant).
- **Technical Setup:** Modern browser, 8+ GB RAM, ability to upload/download CSV, PDF, DOCX, and PNG files.
- **Data:** Use only provided synthetic/redacted datasets; no confidential or PII/PHI data.
- **Time Commitment:** 24 contact hours plus 2 optional practice hours.
- **Fallbacks:** Decision trees and simulated workflows provided if any feature is unavailable.

---

## 1. LLM Fundamentals & Prompt Mechanics

### Description:
Introduces the foundational concepts of large language models (LLMs) and prompt engineering. Participants learn to structure effective prompts using the RTCFCE (Role, Task, Context, Format, Constraints, Evaluation) framework, analyze model responses, and understand how prompt design impacts output quality. This session establishes a baseline for all subsequent modules.

### Takeaways:
- Ability to craft precise, context-rich prompts for diverse business tasks
- Understanding of how prompt structure affects LLM outputs
- Skills to evaluate and iterate on prompt effectiveness

### Learning Goals:
- Apply the RTCFCE framework to design prompts for specific business scenarios
- Analyze and compare model outputs for accuracy, clarity, and constraint adherence
- Develop a rationale matrix to justify prompt design choices

### Exercise Description:
Duration: 60 minutes, Debrief: 20 minutes  
**Tools/Features:** ChatGPT Prompting, RTCFCE framework, Custom Instructions (preview)

---

## 2. Context Engineering & Memory/Custom Instructions

### Description:
Explores strategies for providing context to ChatGPT, including the use of Custom Instructions and Memory features. Participants personalize outputs, compare results with and without context, and manage domain-specific glossaries. The session also covers privacy considerations and fallback strategies when features are unavailable.

### Takeaways:
- Mastery of Custom Instructions for tailored outputs
- Techniques for context management and memory utilization
- Awareness of privacy and data handling best practices

### Learning Goals:
- Configure and apply Custom Instructions to personalize ChatGPT responses
- Evaluate the impact of context on output quality through before/after comparisons
- Create and use domain glossaries to enhance task relevance
- Implement decision trees for feature availability and fallbacks

### Exercise Description:
Duration: 60 minutes, Debrief: 20 minutes  
**Tools/Features:** Custom Instructions, Memory, File Uploads, Decision Trees

---

## 3. Web-Grounded Research with Browse

### Description:
Leverages ChatGPT’s Browse feature to conduct web-based research, collect recent and reputable sources, and critically assess citation quality. Emphasizes safe sourcing, citation management, and synthesizing findings into actionable business briefs.

### Takeaways:
- Proficiency in using Browse for up-to-date, web-grounded research
- Skills in evaluating source credibility and recency
- Ability to synthesize and cite information in business deliverables

### Learning Goals:
- Use Browse to identify and extract information from reputable sources
- Evaluate and document citation quality and recency
- Produce structured research briefs with proper citations

### Exercise Description:
Duration: 60 minutes, Debrief: 20 minutes  
**Tools/Features:** Browse, Citations, Source QA

---

## 4. Advanced Data Analysis (ADA) for Pros

### Description:
Focuses on ChatGPT’s Advanced Data Analysis (ADA) capabilities, including multi-file uploads, KPI computation, and data visualization. Participants analyze synthetic datasets, generate charts, and narrate findings, with attention to data validation and fallback strategies.

### Takeaways:
- Competence in uploading and analyzing multiple CSV files
- Ability to define and visualize key business metrics
- Experience in validating and narrating data-driven insights

### Learning Goals:
- Upload and join multiple datasets for analysis
- Compute KPIs and generate visualizations using ADA
- Narrate findings and flag data anomalies
- Apply fallback methods when ADA is unavailable

### Exercise Description:
Duration: 60 minutes, Debrief: 20 minutes  
**Tools/Features:** Advanced Data Analysis (ADA), Multi-file Uploads, Data Visualization

---

## 5. Document & Table Intelligence (Vision)

### Description:
Uses ChatGPT’s Vision features to extract structured data from documents and images, correct OCR errors, and validate outputs. Covers best practices for handling complex tables, image skew, and privacy considerations in document processing.

### Takeaways:
- Proficiency in extracting tables from PDFs and images using Vision
- Skills in identifying and correcting OCR/structure errors
- Understanding of validation workflows and privacy safeguards

### Learning Goals:
- Extract and validate tables from diverse document formats
- Log and correct OCR/structure errors
- Produce QA checklists and error logs for document analysis

### Exercise Description:
Duration: 60 minutes, Debrief: 20 minutes  
**Tools/Features:** Vision (PDF/Table/Image), File Uploads, ADA (Validation)

---

## 6. Image Generation & Multimodal Workflows

### Description:
Introduces image generation and multimodal workflows, integrating brand guidelines and accessibility requirements. Participants create, refine, and assess images for business use, ensuring alignment with brand and accessibility standards.

### Takeaways:
- Ability to generate and refine images using ChatGPT’s image tools
- Skills in applying brand and accessibility constraints to creative outputs

### Learning Goals:
- Extract brand constraints from provided guides using Vision

- Assess outputs for brand fit and accessibility compliance

### Exercise Description:
Duration: 60 minutes, Debrief: 20 minutes  
**Tools/Features:** Image Generation, Vision, Accessibility Checklists

## 7. Custom GPTs: Builder, Knowledge, No-Code Actions (Simulated)

### Description:
Guides participants through designing and testing Custom GPTs using the Builder, Knowledge files, and simulated no-code Actions. Covers system prompt engineering, retrieval quality, and the process of preparing GPTs for internal or store deployment, including fallback strategies.

### Takeaways:
- Mastery of Custom GPT Builder and Knowledge file integration
- Skills in designing system prompts and retrieval guardrails
- Understanding of no-code Actions and GPT Store listing requirements

### Learning Goals:
- Build and test a Custom GPT with Knowledge files
- Design system prompts for accurate, grounded responses
- Simulate no-code Actions and prepare store listing documentation
- Apply fallback strategies if Builder is unavailable

### Exercise Description:
Duration: 60 minutes, Debrief: 20 minutes  
**Tools/Features:** Custom GPT Builder, Knowledge Files, GPT Store (Mock), Simulated Actions

---

## 8. Teams/Enterprise Governance & Capstone

### Description:
Addresses governance, privacy, and compliance in ChatGPT Teams/Enterprise environments. Participants execute a capstone workflow, integrating multiple tools, and produce a standard operating procedure (SOP) with governance checklists for real-world deployment.

### Takeaways:
- Understanding of Teams/Enterprise data controls and workspace management
- Ability to design compliant, end-to-end AI workflows
- Experience in producing SOPs and governance documentation

### Learning Goals:
- Configure Teams/Enterprise settings for privacy and compliance
- Execute a multi-tool capstone workflow with artifact production
- Develop SOPs and governance checklists for workflow deployment
- Present and defend their workflow in a peer review

### Exercise Description:
Duration: 60 minutes, Debrief: 30 minutes  
**Tools/Features:** Teams/Enterprise Features, Privacy Controls, Shared Workspaces
