# ChatGPT Pro Power User: No-Code Productivity and Analysis

### Overview:
This intensive 24-hour course is designed for cross-functional professionals aiming to master ChatGPT’s advanced no-code productivity and analysis features. Participants will progress from foundational prompt engineering to medium-advanced applications, including data analysis, web research, document intelligence, image generation, and custom GPT workflows. The course emphasizes hands-on labs, artifact-based assessments, and governance best practices, enabling learners to confidently integrate ChatGPT Pro into real-world business scenarios—no coding required.

---

## 1. LLM Fundamentals & Prompt Mechanics

### Description:
This module introduces the foundational concepts of large language models (LLMs) and prompt engineering. Participants will learn to structure effective prompts using the RTCFCE (Role, Task, Context, Format, Constraints, Evaluation) framework, analyze model responses, and understand how prompt design impacts output quality. The session establishes a baseline for all subsequent modules.

### Takeaways:
- Ability to craft precise, context-rich prompts for diverse business tasks
- Understanding of how prompt structure affects LLM outputs
- Skills to evaluate and iterate on prompt effectiveness

### Learning Goals:
- Participants apply the RTCFCE framework to design prompts for specific business scenarios.
- Participants analyze and compare model outputs for accuracy, clarity, and constraint adherence.
- Participants develop a rationale matrix to justify prompt design choices.

### Exercise Description:
Duration: 60 minutes, Debrief: 20 minutes  
**Tools/Features:** ChatGPT Prompting, RTCFCE framework, Custom Instructions (preview)

---

## 2. Context Engineering & Memory/Custom Instructions

### Description:
This module explores strategies for providing context to ChatGPT, including the use of Custom Instructions and Memory features. Participants will personalize outputs, compare results with and without context, and learn to manage domain-specific glossaries. The session also covers privacy considerations and fallback strategies when features are unavailable.

### Takeaways:
- Mastery of Custom Instructions for tailored outputs
- Techniques for context management and memory utilization
- Awareness of privacy and data handling best practices

### Learning Goals:
- Participants configure and apply Custom Instructions to personalize ChatGPT responses.
- Participants evaluate the impact of context on output quality through before/after comparisons.
- Participants create and use domain glossaries to enhance task relevance.
- Participants implement decision trees for feature availability and fallbacks.

### Exercise Description:
Duration: 60 minutes, Debrief: 20 minutes  
**Tools/Features:** Custom Instructions, Memory, File Uploads, Decision Trees

---

## 3. Web-Grounded Research with Browse

### Description:
Participants will leverage ChatGPT’s Browse feature to conduct web-based research, collect recent and reputable sources, and critically assess citation quality. The module emphasizes safe sourcing, citation management, and synthesizing findings into actionable business briefs.

### Takeaways:
- Proficiency in using Browse for up-to-date, web-grounded research
- Skills in evaluating source credibility and recency
- Ability to synthesize and cite information in business deliverables

### Learning Goals:
- Participants use Browse to identify and extract information from reputable sources.
- Participants evaluate and document citation quality and recency.
- Participants produce structured research briefs with proper citations.

### Exercise Description:
Duration: 60 minutes, Debrief: 20 minutes  
**Tools/Features:** Browse, Citations, Source QA

---

## 4. Advanced Data Analysis (ADA) for Pros

### Description:
This module focuses on ChatGPT’s Advanced Data Analysis (ADA) capabilities, including multi-file uploads, KPI computation, and data visualization. Participants will analyze synthetic datasets, generate charts, and narrate findings, with attention to data validation and fallback strategies.

### Takeaways:
- Competence in uploading and analyzing multiple CSV files
- Ability to define and visualize key business metrics
- Experience in validating and narrating data-driven insights

### Learning Goals:
- Participants upload and join multiple datasets for analysis.
- Participants compute KPIs and generate visualizations using ADA.
- Participants narrate findings and flag data anomalies.
- Participants apply fallback methods when ADA is unavailable.

### Exercise Description:
Duration: 60 minutes, Debrief: 20 minutes  
**Tools/Features:** Advanced Data Analysis (ADA), Multi-file Uploads, Data Visualization

---

## 5. Document & Table Intelligence (Vision)

### Description:
Participants will use ChatGPT’s Vision features to extract structured data from documents and images, correct OCR errors, and validate outputs. The module covers best practices for handling complex tables, image skew, and privacy considerations in document processing.

### Takeaways:
- Proficiency in extracting tables from PDFs and images using Vision
- Skills in identifying and correcting OCR/structure errors
- Understanding of validation workflows and privacy safeguards

### Learning Goals:
- Participants extract and validate tables from diverse document formats.
- Participants log and correct OCR/structure errors.
- Participants produce QA checklists and error logs for document analysis.

### Exercise Description:
Duration: 60 minutes, Debrief: 20 minutes  
**Tools/Features:** Vision (PDF/Table/Image), File Uploads, ADA (Validation)

---

## 6. Image Generation & Multimodal Workflows

### Description:
This module introduces image generation and multimodal workflows, integrating brand guidelines and accessibility requirements. Participants will create, refine, and assess images for business use, ensuring alignment with brand and accessibility standards.

### Takeaways:
- Ability to generate and refine images using ChatGPT’s image tools
- Skills in applying brand and accessibility constraints to creative outputs
- Experience in multimodal prompt iteration and evaluation

### Learning Goals:
- Participants extract brand constraints from provided guides using Vision.
- Participants generate and refine images to meet business briefs.
- Participants assess outputs for brand fit and accessibility compliance.

### Exercise Description:
Duration: 60 minutes, Debrief: 20 minutes  
**Tools/Features:** Image Generation, Vision, Accessibility Checklists

---

## 7. Custom GPTs: Builder, Knowledge, No-Code Actions (Simulated)

### Description:
Participants will design and test Custom GPTs using the Builder, Knowledge files, and simulated no-code Actions. The module covers system prompt engineering, retrieval quality, and the process of preparing GPTs for internal or store deployment, including fallback strategies.

### Takeaways:
- Mastery of Custom GPT Builder and Knowledge file integration
- Skills in designing system prompts and retrieval guardrails
- Understanding of no-code Actions and GPT Store listing requirements

### Learning Goals:
- Participants build and test a Custom GPT with Knowledge files.
- Participants design system prompts for accurate, grounded responses.
- Participants simulate no-code Actions and prepare store listing documentation.
- Participants apply fallback strategies if Builder is unavailable.

### Exercise Description:
Duration: 60 minutes, Debrief: 20 minutes  
**Tools/Features:** Custom GPT Builder, Knowledge Files, GPT Store (Mock), Simulated Actions

---

## 8. Teams/Enterprise Governance & Capstone

### Description:
The final module addresses governance, privacy, and compliance in ChatGPT Teams/Enterprise environments. Participants will execute a capstone workflow, integrating multiple tools, and produce a standard operating procedure (SOP) with governance checklists for real-world deployment.

### Takeaways:
- Understanding of Teams/Enterprise data controls and workspace management
- Ability to design compliant, end-to-end AI workflows
- Experience in producing SOPs and governance documentation

### Learning Goals:
- Participants configure Teams/Enterprise settings for privacy and compliance.
- Participants execute a multi-tool capstone workflow with artifact production.
- Participants develop SOPs and governance checklists for workflow deployment.
- Participants present and defend their workflow in a peer review.

### Exercise Description:
Duration: 60 minutes, Debrief: 30 minutes  
**Tools/Features:** Teams/Enterprise Features, Privacy Controls, Shared Workspaces
