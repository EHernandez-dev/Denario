## Prerequisites for "ChatGPT Tools Unleashed: A Professional's Guide to Advanced Features"

### Overview

To ensure participants can fully engage with the course content and activities, a baseline of digital literacy and professional experience is required. The prerequisites are designed to be accessible to a broad range of professionals, with no expectation of coding or technical AI expertise.

### Required Knowledge and Experience

- **Familiarity with Digital Communication Platforms**
  - Participants should have experience using common workplace communication tools such as Slack, Microsoft Teams, Zoom, or Google Meet.
  - Example: Regularly sending messages, sharing files, or participating in group chats or video calls.

- **Basic Use of Productivity Tools**
  - Comfort with standard productivity software, including word processors (e.g., Microsoft Word, Google Docs), spreadsheets (e.g., Excel, Google Sheets), and cloud storage (e.g., Google Drive, Dropbox).
  - Example: Creating, editing, and sharing documents or spreadsheets as part of daily work tasks.

- **Experience with Web-Based Applications**
  - Ability to navigate web browsers, manage tabs, and interact with web-based platforms.
  - Example: Using online project management tools (e.g., Trello, Asana) or customer relationship management (CRM) systems.

- **Basic Experience with Chatbots or Virtual Assistants (Preferred, Not Required)**
  - Prior interaction with chatbots (e.g., website support bots, virtual assistants like Siri or Alexa) is helpful but not mandatory.
  - Example: Using a chatbot to get information or complete a simple task online.

- **General Professional Communication Skills**
  - Ability to articulate questions, provide feedback, and engage in collaborative problem-solving in a professional context.

### No Technical or Coding Background Required

- The course is explicitly designed for non-technical professionals. No prior experience with programming, scripting, or AI model configuration is necessary.

### Summary Table: Prerequisite Examples

| Area                        | Example(s)                                                      |

These prerequisites ensure that all participants can focus on mastering ChatGPT tools and features without being hindered by basic digital skills gaps. The course will provide all necessary onboarding for ChatGPT-specific interfaces and workflows.
