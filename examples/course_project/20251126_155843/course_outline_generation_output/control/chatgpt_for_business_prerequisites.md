## Prerequisites for "ChatGPT for Business: A Comprehensive Guide to Professional Proficiency"

### Required Knowledge and Experience

- **General Business Acumen:**  
  Participants should possess a foundational understanding of business operations, including familiarity with common workflows such as email communication, report generation, and meeting management. This ensures relevance and immediate applicability of course content.

- **Digital Literacy:**  
  Basic proficiency in using web-based applications, navigating user interfaces, and managing files (uploading, downloading, organizing) is necessary. Participants should be comfortable with standard productivity tools (e.g., Microsoft Office, Google Workspace).

- **No Prior Coding or AI Expertise Required:**  
  The course is designed for non-technical professionals. No programming or advanced AI knowledge is assumed.

- **Interest in Process Improvement and Innovation:**  
  Participants should be open to adopting new technologies and exploring ways to enhance efficiency and productivity within their business context.

### Recommended (but Not Mandatory) Background

- **Experience with Collaboration Tools:**  
  Familiarity with platforms such as Slack, Microsoft Teams, or project management tools (e.g., Trello, Asana) will facilitate understanding of integration scenarios.

- **Exposure to Automation Concepts:**  
  While not required, prior exposure to basic automation (e.g., using rules in email clients or simple workflow automations in tools like Zapier) will accelerate learning in advanced modules.
