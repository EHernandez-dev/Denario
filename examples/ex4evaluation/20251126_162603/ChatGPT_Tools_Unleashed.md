# ChatGPT Tools Unleashed: Navigating Advanced Features

### Overview:
This one-day, hands-on course is designed for professionals who want to integrate ChatGPT’s advanced features—such as memory, custom instructions, and tool integrations—into their daily work. No technical or coding background is required. Across eight scaffolded modules, participants will learn to personalize, operationalize, and ethically deploy ChatGPT for greater productivity and impact in professional workflows.

---

## 1. Introduction to ChatGPT’s Professional Ecosystem

### Description:
This module orients participants to the evolving landscape of ChatGPT in professional settings. It introduces the course structure, learning objectives, and the spectrum of ChatGPT’s features, setting the stage for deeper exploration.

### Takeaways:
- Clear understanding of ChatGPT’s role in modern professional workflows
- Awareness of the full range of ChatGPT features and tools
- Clarity on course structure and learning outcomes

### Learning Goals:
- Participants articulate the value of ChatGPT in professional contexts.
- Participants identify key features and tools available in ChatGPT.
- Participants outline the course roadmap and set personal learning objectives.

### Exercise Description:
Duration: 10 minutes (interactive poll and group discussion), Debrief: 5 minutes
