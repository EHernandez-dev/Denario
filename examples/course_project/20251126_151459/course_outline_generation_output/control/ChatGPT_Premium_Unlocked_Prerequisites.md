## Prerequisites for "ChatGPT Premium Unlocked: Tools and Techniques for Business Excellence"

### Foundational Knowledge and Experience

- **General Digital Literacy:** Participants should be comfortable navigating web-based applications, managing files, and using productivity tools (e.g., spreadsheets, document editors).
- **Familiarity with Business Processes:** A basic understanding of common business workflows (such as customer support, project management, or data analysis) is recommended to contextualize AI applications.
- **Awareness of AI Concepts:** While no deep technical expertise is required, participants should possess a general awareness of what AI is, its typical business uses, and its limitations. Prior hands-on experience with ChatGPT (free or basic version) is helpful but not mandatory.

### Required Familiarity

- **No Coding Required:** The course is designed for non-technical professionals; coding or scripting experience is not necessary.
- **Exposure to Business Tools:** Experience with digital business tools (e.g., CRM systems, ticketing platforms, or analytics dashboards) will help participants relate course content to their work environments.
- **Case Study Analysis:** Some prior exposure to analyzing business case studies or scenarios is beneficial, as the course includes interactive workshops and real-world case analysis.

### Soft Skills

- **Critical Thinking:** Ability to evaluate information, question assumptions, and assess the relevance and reliability of AI-generated outputs.
- **Communication Skills:** Willingness to participate in group discussions, share insights, and articulate business needs or challenges.
- **Collaboration:** Openness to working in small teams during workshops and simulation exercises.
