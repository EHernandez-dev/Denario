## Prerequisites for "AI-Driven Business Solutions: Leveraging ChatGPT Effectively"

### Intended Audience Alignment

The course is tailored for non-technical professionals who seek to leverage ChatGPT for business problem-solving. The curriculum assumes no prior experience with artificial intelligence, programming, or data science. However, participants are expected to possess foundational business knowledge and a willingness to engage with new digital tools in a professional context.

### Detailed Prerequisites

#### 1. General Business Acumen
- **Requirement:** Familiarity with basic business operations, terminology, and workflows.
- **Rationale:** Participants should be able to contextualize AI applications within their own business environments, such as marketing, sales, customer service, or project management.

#### 2. Digital Literacy
- **Requirement:** Comfort with standard office productivity tools (e.g., email, word processing, spreadsheets) and web-based applications.
- **Rationale:** The course involves hands-on exercises using ChatGPT interfaces and may require navigating online platforms or copying/pasting data.

#### 3. Problem-Solving Mindset
- **Requirement:** Ability to identify and articulate business challenges or inefficiencies.
- **Rationale:** Participants will be expected to map real-world business problems to potential AI-driven solutions and evaluate the impact of those solutions.

#### 4. Communication Skills
- **Requirement:** Proficiency in written and verbal business communication.
- **Rationale:** Effective prompt engineering and evaluation of AI outputs depend on clear, concise communication and critical review.

#### 5. No Prior AI or Programming Experience Required
- **Clarification:** The course is explicitly designed for those without a technical background. All AI concepts, terminology, and tools will be introduced from first principles.

### Recommended (But Not Required) Experience

- **Exposure to Business Process Improvement:** Experience with initiatives such as Lean, Six Sigma, or digital transformation may enhance participants’ ability to identify high-impact AI use cases.
- **Interest in Innovation or Change Management:** A proactive attitude toward adopting new technologies will facilitate deeper engagement with course content.

### Alignment with Course Expectations

These prerequisites ensure that participants can fully engage with the course’s practical, business-focused approach to AI. The course will scaffold all technical concepts, focusing on actionable skills and real-world application rather than theoretical or technical depth. This alignment maximizes accessibility while ensuring that learning outcomes are relevant and immediately applicable in a business context.
