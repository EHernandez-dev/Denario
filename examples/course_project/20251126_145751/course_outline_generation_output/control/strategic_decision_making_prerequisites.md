## Prerequisites for "Strategic Decision Making with ChatGPT: From Basics to Breakthroughs"

### General Knowledge and Experience

- **Business Acumen:**  
  Participants should possess a foundational understanding of business operations, strategic planning, and decision-making processes. Familiarity with concepts such as market analysis, SWOT, and competitive intelligence is recommended but not mandatory.

- **Professional Experience:**  
  The course is designed for business professionals, managers, analysts, and decision-makers. Prior experience in roles involving strategic planning, business analysis, or management will enhance the learning experience.

### Technical Prerequisites

- **No Coding Required:**  
  The course is explicitly structured for non-coders. No programming, scripting, or advanced technical skills are necessary.

- **Basic Digital Literacy:**  
  Participants should be comfortable using web browsers, navigating online platforms, and working with common business productivity tools (e.g., spreadsheets, document editors).

- **ChatGPT Account:**  
  Attendees should have access to an OpenAI ChatGPT account. For full participation in all exercises, access to both the free (GPT-3.5) and premium (GPT-4, Advanced Data Analysis, web browsing, plugins) features is recommended. Guidance will be provided on account setup prior to the course.

### Accessibility Considerations

- **No Prior AI Experience Needed:**  
  The course assumes no prior experience with artificial intelligence, machine learning, or large language models.

- **Support for Diverse Backgrounds:**  
  All technical concepts will be introduced from first principles, ensuring accessibility for participants from varied professional and educational backgrounds.
