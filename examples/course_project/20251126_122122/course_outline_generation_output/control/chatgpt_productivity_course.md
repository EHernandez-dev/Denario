# Proposed Main Modules for "Enhancing Productivity with ChatGPT Tools"

A scaffolded, 8-hour course for business professionals should be structured to move from foundational understanding and tool orientation, through practical application, to advanced integration and critical evaluation. The following six modules are proposed, each building on the previous, with estimated durations to ensure a balanced and engaging learning experience.

---

## 1. Introduction to ChatGPT and AI Productivity Tools  
**Duration:** 45 minutes  
**Focus:**  
- Overview of generative AI and ChatGPT  
- Key concepts, terminology, and business relevance  
- Setting expectations for productivity enhancement

---

## 2. Navigating ChatGPT: Interface, Features, and Access  
**Duration:** 1 hour  
**Focus:**  
- Hands-on orientation to ChatGPTâ€™s interface  
- Exploration of core features (chat, history, settings, templates)  
- Account setup and best practices for secure, effective use

---

## 3. Prompt Engineering Fundamentals for Business Tasks  
**Duration:** 1 hour 15 minutes  
**Focus:**  
- Principles of crafting effective prompts  
- Iterative refinement and troubleshooting  
- Introduction to prompt templates for common business scenarios

---

## 4. Applying ChatGPT to Daily Business Operations  
**Duration:** 2 hours  
**Focus:**  
- Practical, hands-on exercises using ChatGPT for real-world tasks:  
  - Email drafting  
  - Meeting summaries  
  - Research and information synthesis  
  - Report and content generation  
- Group activities to simulate collaborative workflows

---

## 5. Advanced Features and Workflow Integration  
**Duration:** 1 hour 30 minutes  
**Focus:**  
- Leveraging advanced ChatGPT features (custom instructions, memory, plugins/integrations)  
- Embedding ChatGPT into existing business processes  
- Identifying automation opportunities and evaluating tool fit

---

## 6. Critical Evaluation, Ethics, and Best Practices  
**Duration:** 1 hour 30 minutes  
**Focus:**  
- Assessing output quality, accuracy, and relevance  
- Recognizing limitations, biases, and ethical considerations  
- Strategies for responsible, secure, and collaborative use in business settings  
- Group reflection and action planning for ongoing adoption

---

**Total Duration:** 8 hours

This modular structure ensures a logical progression from foundational knowledge to advanced application, with ample time for hands-on practice, group exercises, and critical reflection. Each module is designed to be self-contained yet interdependent, supporting cumulative skill development and immediate workplace applicability.
