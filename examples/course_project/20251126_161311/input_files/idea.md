
Course Idea:
	* ChatGPT Proficiency for Non-Coders: From Basics to Breakthroughs
		- This course is designed for professionals seeking to master ChatGPT without the need for coding skills.
		- Participants will begin with foundational concepts such as language models and prompt crafting, progressing to advanced features like plugins and extensions.
		- The course includes interactive labs and group discussions, providing a hands-on approach to experimenting with ChatGPT's capabilities in real-world scenarios.
		- A strategic plan will be developed to enhance professional productivity, with clear, actionable steps and a refined technical roadmap.
		- The course will feature module examples and case study walkthroughs, offering a comprehensive learning experience that balances theory with practical application.
