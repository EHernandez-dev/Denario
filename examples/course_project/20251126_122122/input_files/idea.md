
Course Idea:
	* Enhancing Productivity with ChatGPT Tools
		- This course is designed to empower business professionals by teaching them how to effectively use ChatGPT's advanced features to boost productivity.
		- Participants will learn simplified explanations of complex tools, ensuring clarity and accessibility for those without a technical background.
		- The curriculum is streamlined to focus on practical applications, with clear pacing to fit within the 8-hour timeframe.
		- The course includes tangible examples of daily business operations where ChatGPT can directly enhance productivity, providing actionable skills.
		- Delivery methods such as hands-on practice, group exercises, and guided walkthroughs are incorporated to enhance engagement and ensure participants can immediately apply what they learn in their daily operations.
