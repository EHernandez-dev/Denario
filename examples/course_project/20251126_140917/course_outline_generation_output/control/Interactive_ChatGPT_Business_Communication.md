# Core Competencies and Skills for "Interactive ChatGPT: Enhancing Business Communication and Collaboration"

## Overview

The course is designed to empower business professionals to strategically leverage ChatGPT for improved communication and collaboration. The focus is on both foundational and advanced competencies, including prompt engineering, context management, responsible AI use, and practical application in real-world business scenarios. The following analysis identifies the essential competencies and skills, and defines evaluation criteria for each to ensure alignment with business outcomes.

---

## Essential Competencies and Skills

### 1. Foundational Understanding of ChatGPT and AI in Business Communication

- **Skill:** Recognize the capabilities, limitations, and appropriate use cases of ChatGPT in business contexts.
- **Evaluation Criteria:**
  - Participants can accurately describe ChatGPTâ€™s core functionalities and limitations.
  - Participants can identify at least three business scenarios where ChatGPT adds value.
  - Participants can distinguish between tasks suitable and unsuitable for AI-driven communication.

### 2. Prompt Crafting and Engineering

- **Skill:** Formulate effective prompts to elicit desired responses from ChatGPT.
- **Evaluation Criteria:**
  - Participants can create clear, context-rich prompts for various business tasks.
  - Participants can compare and evaluate the effectiveness of different prompt structures.
  - Participants can demonstrate iterative refinement of prompts to improve output quality.

### 3. Context Management and Conversation Structuring

- **Skill:** Manage and maintain context in multi-turn conversations with ChatGPT for coherent, relevant outputs.
- **Evaluation Criteria:**
  - Participants can explain the importance of context in AI-driven dialogues.
  - Participants can apply strategies to maintain context across multiple exchanges.
  - Participants can identify and correct breakdowns in context during simulated interactions.

### 4. Application to Real-World Business Scenarios

- **Skill:** Apply ChatGPT to solve practical business communication and collaboration challenges.
- **Evaluation Criteria:**
  - Participants can select appropriate ChatGPT features for specific business scenarios (e.g., drafting emails, summarizing meetings, brainstorming).
  - Participants can demonstrate the use of ChatGPT in group activities and role-playing exercises.
  - Participants can evaluate the impact of ChatGPT-assisted communication on workflow efficiency.

### 5. Advanced Features and Customization

- **Skill:** Navigate and utilize advanced ChatGPT features (e.g., custom instructions, integrations, plugins) for enhanced business outcomes.
- **Evaluation Criteria:**
  - Participants can describe and demonstrate the use of advanced ChatGPT functionalities.
  - Participants can formulate strategies for integrating ChatGPT with existing business tools.
  - Participants can create custom workflows or templates for recurring business tasks.

### 6. Responsible AI Use, Trust, and Accountability

- **Skill:** Apply ethical principles, ensure responsible use, and foster trust in AI-driven business communication.
- **Evaluation Criteria:**
  - Participants can identify potential risks and biases in AI-generated content.
  - Participants can explain accountability measures and best practices for responsible AI use.
  - Participants can verify and validate AI outputs for accuracy and appropriateness in business contexts.

### 7. Collaborative and Interactive Skills

- **Skill:** Engage in group activities, role-playing, and peer feedback to enhance learning and practical application.
- **Evaluation Criteria:**
  - Participants can actively participate in collaborative exercises and role-plays.
  - Participants can provide constructive feedback to peers on prompt design and AI interaction.
  - Participants can reflect on group outcomes to identify areas for improvement.
