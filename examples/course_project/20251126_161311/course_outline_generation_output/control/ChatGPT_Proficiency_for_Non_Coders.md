# ChatGPT Proficiency for Non-Coders: From Basics to Breakthroughs

### Overview:
This hands-on course is designed for professionals who want to master ChatGPT for productivity, creativity, and collaboration—without any coding required. Participants will progress from foundational concepts of conversational AI and prompt engineering to advanced features such as plugins, extensions, and strategic integration into daily workflows. Through interactive labs, real-world case studies, and group discussions, learners will develop actionable skills to leverage ChatGPT effectively and responsibly in diverse professional contexts.

---

## 1. Introduction to ChatGPT and Conversational AI

### Description:
This module introduces the fundamentals of large language models (LLMs) and conversational AI, focusing on ChatGPT’s capabilities, limitations, and real-world impact. Participants will explore how AI is transforming professional environments, illustrated by case studies such as customer support automation and content generation in marketing.

### Takeaways:
- Clear understanding of what ChatGPT is and how it works.
- Awareness of AI’s role and limitations in professional settings.
- Ability to articulate the value and risks of conversational AI to colleagues.

### Learning Goals:
- Participants explain the core principles behind ChatGPT and LLMs.
- Participants identify practical applications and limitations of ChatGPT.
- Participants describe real-world examples of AI adoption in various industries.

---

## 2. Navigating the ChatGPT Interface and Core Features

### Description:
This module provides a guided tour of the ChatGPT web interface, including conversation management, session history, settings, and account features. Participants will practice navigating the tool efficiently, with troubleshooting tips and a focus on maximizing productivity. A case study will demonstrate how a project manager uses session history to track brainstorming sessions.

### Takeaways:
- Proficiency in using the ChatGPT interface and managing conversations.
- Confidence in accessing and adjusting settings for optimal use.
- Ability to troubleshoot common interface issues.

### Learning Goals:
- Participants navigate the ChatGPT interface to initiate and manage sessions.
- Participants utilize session history and settings to organize their work.
- Participants troubleshoot basic issues related to account and interface use.

---

## 3. Prompt Engineering: Crafting Effective Inputs

### Description:
This module delves into prompt engineering, teaching participants how to design clear, targeted prompts for high-quality outputs. Through hands-on labs, learners will experiment with prompt structure, context, and specificity. A marketing case study will illustrate how prompt refinement leads to better campaign ideas.

### Takeaways:
- Mastery of prompt crafting techniques for diverse tasks.
- Understanding of how prompt specificity influences AI responses.
- Ability to iteratively refine prompts for improved results.

### Learning Goals:
- Participants construct effective prompts for various professional scenarios.
- Participants analyze and adjust prompts to optimize ChatGPT outputs.
- Participants apply prompt engineering to real-world tasks (e.g., drafting emails, generating reports).

### Exercise Description:
Duration: 25 minutes, Debrief: 10 minutes

---

## 4. Evaluating and Refining AI Outputs

### Description:
This module focuses on critical evaluation of ChatGPT’s responses, including accuracy, reliability, and appropriateness. Participants will learn techniques for iterative refinement and recognize potential biases. A case study from HR will show how output evaluation ensures compliance and quality in policy drafts.

### Takeaways:
- Skills to assess and improve the quality of AI-generated content.
- Awareness of common pitfalls and biases in AI outputs.
- Strategies for iterative refinement of responses.

### Learning Goals:
- Participants evaluate ChatGPT outputs for accuracy and relevance.
- Participants identify and mitigate biases or errors in AI responses.
- Participants implement iterative feedback to enhance output quality.

### Exercise Description:
Duration: 20 minutes, Debrief: 10 minutes

---

## 5. Advanced Features: Plugins, Extensions, and Integrations

### Description:
This module explores advanced ChatGPT capabilities, including enabling and using official plugins (e.g., web browsing, code interpreter), browser extensions, and third-party integrations with tools like Google Workspace and Microsoft Office. A finance case study will demonstrate using the code interpreter plugin for data analysis.

### Takeaways:
- Familiarity with enabling and using ChatGPT plugins and extensions.
- Understanding of how integrations expand ChatGPT’s utility.
- Ability to select and apply the right tools for specific tasks.

### Learning Goals:
- Participants enable and configure ChatGPT plugins and browser extensions.
- Participants integrate ChatGPT with productivity tools for workflow automation.
- Participants select appropriate advanced features for domain-specific challenges.

### Exercise Description:
Duration: 30 minutes, Debrief: 10 minutes

---

## 6. Customizing ChatGPT for Domain-Specific Tasks

### Description:
This module demonstrates how to tailor ChatGPT for specific professional contexts, such as marketing, HR, project management, and customer support. Participants will work through case studies and hands-on labs to adapt prompts and workflows for their own domains.

### Takeaways:
- Ability to customize ChatGPT for industry-specific needs.
- Skills to design workflows that leverage ChatGPT’s strengths.
- Insights into best practices for domain adaptation.

### Learning Goals:
- Participants adapt ChatGPT prompts for their professional domain.
- Participants design workflows incorporating ChatGPT into daily tasks.
- Participants evaluate the effectiveness of domain-specific customizations.

### Exercise Description:
Duration: 25 minutes, Debrief: 10 minutes

---

## 7. Collaborative and Group Use of ChatGPT

### Description:
This module examines strategies for leveraging ChatGPT in team settings, including collaborative brainstorming, co-authoring documents, and facilitating group problem-solving. A product development case study will highlight how teams use ChatGPT to accelerate ideation and documentation.

### Takeaways:
- Techniques for using ChatGPT in collaborative environments.
- Understanding of group workflows and shared document creation.
- Ability to facilitate team-based problem-solving with AI support.

### Learning Goals:
- Participants coordinate group activities using ChatGPT.
- Participants co-author documents and brainstorm ideas collaboratively.
- Participants facilitate group discussions and decision-making with AI assistance.

### Exercise Description:
Duration: 20 minutes, Debrief: 10 minutes

---

## 8. Strategic Planning and Responsible AI Adoption

### Description:
This capstone module guides participants in developing a strategic plan for integrating ChatGPT into personal and organizational workflows. It addresses ethical, privacy, and security considerations, and provides a roadmap for ongoing skill development. A case study from a consulting firm will illustrate responsible AI adoption and measurable productivity gains.

### Takeaways:
- Framework for strategic AI integration in professional settings.
- Awareness of ethical, privacy, and security best practices.
- Actionable roadmap for continued learning and responsible use.

### Learning Goals:
- Participants develop a strategic plan for ChatGPT adoption in their workflow.
- Participants identify and address ethical and privacy considerations.
- Participants set measurable goals for AI-driven productivity improvements.
- Participants outline steps for ongoing skill development and adaptation.

### Exercise Description:
Duration: 30 minutes, Debrief: 15 minutes

---

This course outline ensures a logical, scaffolded progression from foundational understanding to advanced, real-world application, equipping non-coders with the skills and confidence to leverage ChatGPT for professional success. Each module is grounded in practical examples and hands-on activities, fostering both individual mastery and collaborative innovation.
