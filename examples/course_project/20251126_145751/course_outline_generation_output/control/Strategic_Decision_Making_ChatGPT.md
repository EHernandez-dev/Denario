# Strategic Decision Making with ChatGPT: From Basics to Breakthroughs

### Overview:
This two-day, hands-on course is designed for business professionals eager to leverage ChatGPT for strategic decision-making. No coding experience is required. Through a scaffolded curriculum, participants will progress from foundational concepts of large language models (LLMs) and ChatGPT to advanced, real-world applications in business strategy, market analysis, and responsible AI use. The course features practical exercises, case studies, and a focus on actionable frameworks, with explicit attention to the differences between free and premium ChatGPT features.

---

## Day 1: Foundations and Core Applications

---

## 1. Introduction to ChatGPT and Large Language Models

### Description:
This module introduces the fundamentals of ChatGPT and large language models, demystifying how they work and their relevance to business strategy. Participants will explore the capabilities and limitations of both free and premium versions, setting the stage for effective and responsible use.

### Takeaways:
- Clear understanding of LLMs and ChatGPT’s core functionalities
- Ability to distinguish between free and premium ChatGPT features
- Awareness of the potential and boundaries of AI in business contexts

### Learning Goals:
- Participants identify the basic architecture and operation of ChatGPT.
- Participants distinguish between free and premium ChatGPT features (e.g., GPT-3.5 vs. GPT-4, Advanced Data Analysis, web browsing).
- Participants explain the strengths and limitations of LLMs in business decision-making.

### Exercise Description:
Duration: 20 minutes, Debrief: 10 minutes  
Hands-on exploration: Participants compare outputs from free and premium ChatGPT on a simple business query.

---

## 2. Prompt Engineering for Strategic Outcomes

### Description:
This session focuses on the art and science of crafting effective prompts to elicit high-quality, actionable responses from ChatGPT. Participants will learn prompt design techniques tailored to strategic business scenarios.

### Takeaways:
- Mastery of prompt engineering basics for business use
- Ability to structure queries for clarity and relevance
- Understanding of prompt iteration and refinement

### Learning Goals:
- Participants apply prompt engineering techniques to generate strategic insights.
- Participants formulate prompts for scenario analysis, market research, and planning.
- Participants evaluate and refine prompts for improved output quality.

### Exercise Description:
Duration: 30 minutes, Debrief: 10 minutes  
Participants design, test, and iterate prompts for a business case (e.g., market entry analysis).

---

## 3. Core ChatGPT Tools and Features for Business

### Description:
This module provides a guided tour of ChatGPT’s key tools and features relevant to business professionals, including Advanced Data Analysis (formerly Code Interpreter), web browsing, file uploads, and integration with third-party plugins.

### Takeaways:
- Familiarity with ChatGPT’s business-relevant tools and interfaces
- Ability to select appropriate features for specific strategic tasks
- Understanding of the workflow differences between free and premium versions

### Learning Goals:
- Participants navigate the ChatGPT interface and access core features.
- Participants select and utilize Advanced Data Analysis, web browsing, and file upload functionalities.
- Participants compare the utility of plugins and integrations for business scenarios.

### Exercise Description:
Duration: 25 minutes, Debrief: 10 minutes  
Guided walkthrough: Participants use Advanced Data Analysis to summarize a sample business dataset.

---

## 4. Responsible and Ethical AI Use in Business

### Description:
This session addresses the ethical, legal, and practical considerations of using ChatGPT in strategic decision-making. Topics include data privacy, bias, transparency, and responsible deployment in business contexts.

### Takeaways:
- Awareness of AI ethics and responsible use guidelines
- Ability to recognize and mitigate risks (e.g., bias, data leakage)
- Understanding of compliance and governance best practices

### Learning Goals:
- Participants identify ethical risks and responsibilities in AI use.
- Participants explain data privacy and compliance considerations.
- Participants evaluate outputs for bias and reliability.

### Exercise Description:
Duration: 20 minutes, Debrief: 10 minutes  
Case study analysis: Participants review a scenario involving ethical dilemmas in AI-driven decision-making.

## Day 2: Advanced Applications and Strategic Integration

---

## 5. Market Trend Analysis and Competitive Intelligence with ChatGPT

### Description:
This module demonstrates how to leverage ChatGPT for market trend analysis, competitor benchmarking, and opportunity identification using both built-in features and external data sources.

### Takeaways:
- Practical frameworks for market and competitor analysis
- Ability to synthesize insights from diverse data sources
- Experience using ChatGPT for real-world strategic research

### Learning Goals:
- Participants apply ChatGPT to conduct market trend analysis using web browsing and data upload features.
- Participants create competitor profiles and SWOT analyses with AI assistance.
- Participants evaluate the reliability of AI-generated market insights.

### Exercise Description:
Duration: 35 minutes, Debrief: 15 minutes  
Participants use ChatGPT to analyze a real or simulated market dataset and present findings.

---

## 6. Strategic Planning and Scenario Modeling

### Description:
This session explores how ChatGPT can support strategic planning, scenario modeling, and decision frameworks (e.g., Porter’s Five Forces, PESTLE analysis). Emphasis is placed on structuring complex queries and synthesizing multi-step outputs.

### Takeaways:
- Ability to use ChatGPT for structured strategic planning
- Skills in scenario modeling and risk assessment
- Experience integrating AI outputs into business decision frameworks

### Learning Goals:
- Participants formulate multi-step prompts for scenario modeling.
- Participants apply ChatGPT to generate and compare strategic options.
- Participants demonstrate integration of AI-generated insights into planning documents.

### Exercise Description:
Duration: 40 minutes, Debrief: 15 minutes  
Participants develop a strategic plan for a hypothetical business challenge using ChatGPT.

---

## 7. Case Studies: ChatGPT in Real-World Strategic Decisions

### Description:
This module presents in-depth case studies illustrating successful and unsuccessful uses of ChatGPT in business strategy. Participants will analyze, discuss, and extract best practices and lessons learned.

### Takeaways:
- Exposure to real-world applications and pitfalls
- Ability to critically assess AI’s role in strategic decisions
- Insights into best practices for implementation

### Learning Goals:
- Participants analyze case studies to identify success factors and challenges.
- Participants compare different approaches to integrating ChatGPT in business workflows.
- Participants evaluate the impact of AI-driven decisions on business outcomes.

### Exercise Description:
Duration: 30 minutes, Debrief: 15 minutes  
Group discussion and analysis of provided case studies.

---

## 8. Future Trends and Next Steps with ChatGPT

### Description:
The final module surveys emerging trends in ChatGPT technology, including multimodal capabilities, automation, and integration with enterprise systems. Participants will develop personalized action plans for ongoing learning and adoption.

### Takeaways:
- Awareness of the latest developments in ChatGPT and LLMs
- Understanding of how to stay current and scale AI adoption
- Personalized roadmap for continued skill development

### Learning Goals:
- Participants recognize emerging features (e.g., multimodal input, automation, API integration).
- Participants formulate a plan for ongoing ChatGPT adoption in their organization.
- Participants identify resources for continued learning and support.

### Exercise Description:
Duration: 20 minutes, Debrief: 10 minutes  
Participants draft a personal or team action plan for integrating ChatGPT into their strategic workflows.
