## Prerequisites for "Enhancing Productivity with ChatGPT Tools"

A clear articulation of prerequisites ensures that all participants are adequately prepared to engage with the course content and achieve the intended learning outcomes. The following prerequisites are recommended for participants enrolling in this course:

---

### 1. General Business Experience

**Requirement:**  
Participants should have basic familiarity with daily business operations and workflows.

**Examples:**  
- Experience in roles such as administration, project management, sales, marketing, customer service, or operations.
- Understanding of common business tasks such as email communication, meeting coordination, report writing, and information research.

**Desired Level:**  
No advanced business expertise is required, but participants should be comfortable navigating typical office tasks and processes.

---

### 2. Digital Literacy

**Requirement:**  
Participants should possess foundational digital skills necessary to interact with web-based tools and productivity software.

**Examples:**  
- Proficiency in using web browsers, email clients, and office productivity suites (e.g., Microsoft Office, Google Workspace).
- Ability to navigate online platforms, manage files, and use basic collaboration tools (e.g., Slack, Teams, Zoom).

**Desired Level:**  
Intermediate digital literacy; participants should not require step-by-step instruction for basic computer or internet use.

---

### 3. No Prior AI or ChatGPT Experience Required

**Requirement:**  
No previous experience with AI, machine learning, or ChatGPT is necessary.

**Clarification:**  
- The course is designed to introduce ChatGPT from the ground up, with all technical concepts explained in accessible, non-technical language.
- Participants will be guided through setup, navigation, and use of ChatGPT tools regardless of prior exposure.

---

### 4. English Language Proficiency

**Requirement:**  
Participants should have sufficient proficiency in English to read, write, and communicate effectively during course activities.

**Examples:**  
- Ability to compose business emails, read instructions, and participate in group discussions.

**Desired Level:**  
Intermediate or higher English proficiency to ensure full engagement with course materials and exercises.

---

### 5. Access to Required Technology

**Requirement:**  
Participants must have access to a computer or laptop with a stable internet connection and the ability to access web-based applications.

**Examples:**  
- Ability to log in to ChatGPT via a web browser.
- Capability to participate in virtual or in-person group exercises using digital tools.

---

### Summary Table of Prerequisites

| Area                    | Requirement                                      | Example/Level                        |

---

These prerequisites ensure that all participants can fully benefit from the course, engage in hands-on activities, and immediately apply ChatGPT tools to enhance productivity in their professional contexts.
