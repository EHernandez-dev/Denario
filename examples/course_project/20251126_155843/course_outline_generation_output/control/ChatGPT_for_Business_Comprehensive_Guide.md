# ChatGPT for Business: A Comprehensive Guide to Professional Proficiency

### Overview:
This three-day, hands-on course is designed for business professionals seeking to leverage ChatGPT for enhanced productivity, workflow automation, and strategic decision-making. No technical background is required. Through a scaffolded sequence of ten modules, participants progress from foundational understanding to advanced, real-world application of ChatGPT, including integration into daily operations and the creation of automated business processes using no-code tools.

---

## 1. Foundations of ChatGPT in Business

### Description:
This module introduces the core concepts of ChatGPT, its capabilities, and its limitations within a business context. Participants examine real-world case studies to understand the transformative potential and boundaries of AI-powered language models in various industries.

### Takeaways:
- Clear understanding of ChatGPT’s business applications and constraints
- Awareness of ethical, privacy, and compliance considerations
- Exposure to industry-specific case studies

### Learning Goals:
- Participants describe ChatGPT’s primary functions and limitations.
- Participants identify ethical and compliance issues in business AI use.
- Participants analyze case studies to recognize successful applications.

### Exercise Description:
Duration: 20 minutes, Debrief: 10 minutes  
Case study analysis in small groups, followed by plenary discussion.  
Reflective checkpoint: Identify one potential use and one limitation relevant to your business.

---

## 2. Navigating ChatGPT: Interface, Settings, and Core Features

### Description:
This session provides a guided walkthrough of the ChatGPT interface (web and desktop), focusing on essential features such as prompt history, conversation management, Custom Instructions, and settings for privacy and workflow optimization.

### Takeaways:
- Proficiency in navigating the ChatGPT interface
- Understanding of prompt management and conversation organization
- Ability to customize settings for business needs

### Learning Goals:
- Participants demonstrate navigation of the ChatGPT interface.
- Participants manage and retrieve conversation histories.
- Participants configure Custom Instructions for tailored outputs.

### Exercise Description:
Duration: 15 minutes, Debrief: 5 minutes  
Hands-on exploration of the interface with guided tasks.  
Reflective checkpoint: Adjust Custom Instructions and note the impact on responses.

---

## 3. Prompt Engineering for Business Scenarios

### Description:
This module focuses on crafting effective prompts for diverse business functions, including sales, marketing, HR, and customer support. Emphasis is placed on prompt structuring, context provision, and iterative refinement, with hands-on practice and peer feedback.

### Takeaways:
- Mastery of prompt engineering techniques
- Ability to tailor prompts for specific business functions
- Skills in iterative prompt refinement

### Learning Goals:
- Participants construct clear, goal-oriented prompts.
- Participants adapt prompts to different business domains.
- Participants refine prompts based on output quality.

### Exercise Description:
Duration: 30 minutes, Debrief: 10 minutes  
Individual and group prompt-writing exercises with peer feedback.  
Reflective checkpoint: Share a revised prompt and discuss improvements.

### Description:

### Takeaways:

### Learning Goals:

### Exercise Description:

Role-playing exercises simulating business communications and documentation.  
Reflective checkpoint: Identify a workflow where ChatGPT saved time or improved quality.

### Takeaways:

- Ability to design and deploy basic automated processes

### Learning Goals:
- Participants map business processes suitable for automation.
- Participants connect ChatGPT to external tools via Zapier/Make.
- Participants build and test automated workflows.

Group project: design and implement a simple business automation using a template.  
Reflective checkpoint: Evaluate automation impact and identify next steps for scaling.
