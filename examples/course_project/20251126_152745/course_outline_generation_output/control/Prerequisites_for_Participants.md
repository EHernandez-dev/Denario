## Prerequisites for Participants

### Foundational Knowledge and Experience

- **Basic Digital Literacy:** Participants should be comfortable navigating web-based applications, managing files, and using productivity tools (e.g., email, spreadsheets, document editors).
- **Familiarity with Business Processes:** A working understanding of common business workflows (such as customer support, reporting, or project management) is recommended to contextualize the application of ChatGPT Premium tools.
- **No Prior AI Expertise Required:** The course is designed to be accessible to professionals without a technical background in artificial intelligence or machine learning.

### Recommended Familiarity

- **Experience with Digital Collaboration Tools:** Prior use of platforms like Slack, Microsoft Teams, or project management software (e.g., Asana, Trello) will help participants relate to automation and workflow concepts.
- **Exposure to Data Handling:** While not mandatory, some experience with handling business data (e.g., spreadsheets, basic data analysis) will facilitate engagement with modules on file uploads and data visualization.

### Technical Background and Software Proficiencies

- **No Coding or Chatbot Configuration Required:** The course does not assume programming skills or prior experience configuring chatbots.
- **Optional Advantage:** Participants with experience in configuring digital assistants, automating workflows, or using business intelligence tools may progress more rapidly through advanced modules, but this is not a requirement.

### Summary

The course is suitable for business professionals, team leaders, and digital transformation specialists who are comfortable with digital tools and business processes. It is intentionally designed to be inclusive of non-technical participants, ensuring accessibility while providing opportunities for those with more advanced digital experience to deepen their expertise.
