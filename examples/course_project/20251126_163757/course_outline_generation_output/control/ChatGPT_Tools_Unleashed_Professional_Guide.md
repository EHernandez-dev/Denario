# ChatGPT Tools Unleashed: A Professional's Guide

### Overview:
This intensive 3-day, hands-on course is designed for professionals aiming to master ChatGPT and its ecosystem of tools for workplace productivity and automation. Participants will learn to leverage the ChatGPT API, integrate with productivity suites, and automate workflows using industry-standard platforms such as Zapier and Make.com. Through scaffolded modules, practical exercises, and a capstone project, attendees will gain the skills to implement, evaluate, and optimize ChatGPT-powered solutions in their professional environments.

---

## 1. Introduction to ChatGPT in the Professional Workplace

### Description:
This module introduces ChatGPT’s role in modern professional settings, outlining its capabilities, limitations, and transformative potential. Participants will explore real-world use cases and set personal learning objectives aligned with their roles.

### Takeaways:
- Clear understanding of ChatGPT’s professional applications
- Awareness of course structure and expected outcomes
- Personalized learning goals for the course

### Learning Goals:
- Identify key ChatGPT use cases relevant to professional domains
- Articulate the objectives and structure of the course
- Set individual goals for ChatGPT tool adoption

### Exercise Description:
Duration: 20 minutes, Debrief: 10 minutes  
Interactive case study and group discussion, followed by a short quiz.
