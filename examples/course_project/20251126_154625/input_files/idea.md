
Course Idea:
	* ChatGPT for Business: Leveraging AI for Strategic Advantage
		- This course empowers business professionals to integrate ChatGPT into their decision-making processes, enhancing strategic outcomes.
		- Participants will engage in step-by-step modules and mini-case studies, providing actionable frameworks for both basic and advanced ChatGPT features.
		- The curriculum includes discussions on data privacy, bias minimization, and transparency, ensuring ethical AI use in business contexts.
		- Real-life business scenarios will be incorporated to demonstrate practical applications of ChatGPT tools, offering hands-on experience.
		- By the end of the course, attendees will have a comprehensive understanding of ChatGPT's capabilities, ready to leverage AI for strategic business advantage.
